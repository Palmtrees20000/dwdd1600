The Nintendo World

Welcome to Nintendo Travels, where travelers can explore their favorite in-game Nintendo areas. Have you ever played a game and thought “wouldn't that be great if I could travel there too? Well wonder no longer. As a travel company, we operate to fulfill such wonders and dreams. Nintendo travels offer state of the art diverse options. For example, Feeling tropical? Nintendo Travels can board travelers to their favorite Mario game like “Island Dalfino” from Mario Sunshine. Want to explore the mysteries of a forest? Travel to the Forest Kingdom from Mario Odyssey. Or even a winter getaway? Journey and tour the Snowpeak Ruins with delicious homemade soup guaranteed to replenish strength

As a collaborator to the world renown gaming system who created the Gamecube and Super Nintendo as well as well known games like Super Mario, The legend of Zelda, Pikman, and so much more. Nintendo offers an opportunity to revisit fan favorite sites not on a screen but to step foot and relive precious moments. How is this possible you may ask? Nintendo has many connections that allowed such a dream to come true. As Nintendo demonstrated in various locations with the amusement parks of “Nintendo World”, Nintendo Travels takes Nintendo World to the next level. As an interested traveler begins to book a Nintendo travels flight, a variety of “level ups” or membership packages can be purchased ranging from $999 to $300. Individuals and families will then have the opportunity to visit 3 of their favorite Nintendo locations. One might ask: How many destinations does Nintendo travel offer? Nintendo Travels is updated all the way to the newest games for the Nintendo Switch system. Of course, some games are in the making right now which means new destinations are coming out soon.
